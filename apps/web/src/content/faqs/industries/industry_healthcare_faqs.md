    shortTitle: "Clinical Staff Buy-In"

trust:</strong> Champions become peer advocates among skeptical colleagues</li></ul><h3>Phase 2: Low-Risk Pilots (Weeks 3-6)</h3><ul><li><strong>Start with admin tasks:</strong> Appointment scheduling, follow-up coordination—not clinical decisions</li><li><strong>Show time savings:</strong> 'You got 90 minutes back this week'</li><li><strong>Celebrate wins:</strong> Share success stories Start with physician champions, low-risk pilots, protected learning time, and clear evidence of time savings.</p>"
